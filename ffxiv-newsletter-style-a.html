PATCH NOTES · SQUARE ENIX

Patch 7.2 "Seekers of Eternity" Arrives March 11 — Full Notes Breakdown

The full patch notes for 7.2 have landed, and there's a lot to unpack. The update brings two new Alliance Raid wings of Echoes of Vana'diel, continuation of the main scenario quests, adjustments to Chaotic Alliance Raid rewards, and a sweeping set of Job balance changes that are already sparking debate on the forums.

Healers get notable buffs across the board, while Dragoon sees its Stardiver combo reworked — again. The new Unreal Trial (Ultima's Bane Unreal) goes live same day.
