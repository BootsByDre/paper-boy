Patch Notes

Patch 7.45 Arrives — The Merchant's Tale, Hildibrand, and PvP Shakeups

Patch 7.45 landed on March 3 with a packed content drop. The headline addition is The Merchant's Tale, FFXIV's fourth Variant Dungeon, where you join Y'shtola's sibling Y'nazqha on a branching adventure inside a storybook world. It comes in Variant, Criterion, and a new Advanced difficulty for players who want even more challenge. Alongside it, the Inconceivably Further Hildibrand Adventures continue with a new chapter beginning in Solution Nine, and PvP received a round of job adjustments.

Fan Fest 2026 Contest Submissions Just Closed — Anaheim in April

The Art Contest and Video Contest for Fan Festival 2026 in Anaheim closed submissions on March 6. The main event is April 24–25 at the Anaheim Convention Center, where we'll likely get the next expansion trailer. Winners announced around May 1.

Community

Japanese FFXIV Blog Shuts Down After Square Enix Harassment Settlement

Square Enix reached a settlement with "Netoge Breaking News" (formerly "FF14 Breaking News") over content denigrating FFXIV staff. The blog issued an apology, shut down, and paid an undisclosed amount. Another prominent Japanese blog, "Umadori News," also ceased operations.
